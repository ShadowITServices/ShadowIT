About Shadow IT

Local cyber specialists who speak your language.

Shadow IT works with small and midsize organisations that run on Microsoft 365, cloud apps and a mix of managed service providers and internal IT. Typical clients include professional services, trades, healthcare, non-profits and regional councils.

Instead of enterprise-only frameworks and jargon, we deliver practical controls mapped to the Australian Cyber Security Centre's Small Business and Essential Eight guidance — so you lift security without slowing the business down.
